Greyest Bed

Released November 11, 2016

You spent Christmas
weeping, tripping
over footsteps,
over bones.

I spent New Year's Eve in London,
watched as drunkards sunk a boat.

And so you choked,
and shook our hands.
Each word you spoke,
could break a man.

You felt honest for a moment.
Honesty said, "Leave your home."

You were blinded,
left behind your
royal highness
at the throne.

And so you choked,
and shook our hands.
Each word you spoke,
could break a man.

And you sleep in the greyest bed,
while I sleep alone instead.
