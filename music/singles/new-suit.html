New Suit

Released January 6, 2017

There's really no telling when your whole life can shift completely. When newness strikes it strikes like lightning: all of a sudden. In a flash the world is illuminated again, and thousands of branches of light stem from the origin, thousands of doors opened. But there's a catch: lightning often doesn't strike the same place twice. So move around a little, change your location. Pick up your body, and put it in a new room. Who knows, there may be somebody on the other side who recognizes you; somebody may say "Hello."

I heard a man speak the truth
I saw his heel had a bruise
He adorned me in a New Suit
He led me straight to the room

Where you said, "Hello."
You said, "I think that I know you."
You said, "Hello."
You said, "I think that I know."
